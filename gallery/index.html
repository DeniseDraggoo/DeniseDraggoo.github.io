Body Positive
Book Club

Our Body Positive Book Club is a place where you can connect with other body-positive individuals to network and make new friends in the Seattle area. We choose a different body-positive book every month, but even if you cannot find the time to complete the book, you are still more than welcome to attend our meeting! We meet the 4th Thursday of every month at 7:00pm Pacific Time and are currently meeting online due to lockdowns, but will later return to our north Seattle location.
